# SNS Links

SNS Links aims to make it easy to showcase your web3 brand and identity with all of your social platform links uploaded on chain and shared under one roof! Utilizing the power of SNS domains, and their record functionality you can quickly set up a link sharing page with your SNS domain. Not only can you share social platform links, you can also share all of your cross chain wallet addresses as well. Certain addresses are ownership verifiable, so your frens can have peace of mind that the addresses are legitimate. Made with ❤️ from your friends at Bonfida.
